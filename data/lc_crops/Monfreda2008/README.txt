Ramankutty et al. (2008), "Farming the planet: 1. Geographic distribution of global agricultural lands in the year 2000", Global Biogeochemical Cycles, Vol. 22, GB1003, doi:10.1029/2007GB002952. The data is provided in two formats - NetCDF and ArcINFO ASCII - at 5 minute resolution in latitude by longitude. Hosted at McGill University.
